## Executive Summary

ACME Insight Studio opens a new developer ecosystem that enables ISVs, SI partners, and enterprise teams to build custom AI agents, data connectors, and automation workflows that run natively inside the ACME Cloud productivity suite. This represents a platform shift comparable to the early App Store moment: first movers who build on this platform will capture disproportionate enterprise distribution. Our GTM motion targets three segments (ISV partners, SI implementers, enterprise dev teams) with a developer-first acquisition model, partner co-sell amplification, and a 90-day launch sequence designed to establish category leadership before competitive alternatives mature.

**Target:** 500 published extensions and 2,000 active developers within 6 months of GA.
