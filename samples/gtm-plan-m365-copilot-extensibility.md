## Executive Summary

Microsoft 365 Copilot Extensibility opens a new developer ecosystem that enables ISVs, SIs, and enterprise teams to build custom AI agents, plugins, and connectors that run natively inside M365 Copilot. This represents a platform shift comparable to the early App Store moment: first movers who build on this platform will capture disproportionate enterprise distribution. Our GTM motion targets three segments (ISV partners, SI implementers, enterprise dev teams) with a developer-first acquisition model, partner co-sell amplification, and a 90-day launch sequence designed to establish category leadership before competitive alternatives mature.

**Target:** 500 published extensions and 2,000 active developers within 6 months of GA.
